Our Services

Perpustakaan Negara Malaysia offers a wide range of services to support education, research, and cultural enrichment for all Malaysians.

For the Public

  • Reference Services

    Librarians are available to help users search for information, resources, and references effectively.

  • Adult & Children's Lending

    Borrow fiction, non-fiction, and children's books with ease through our membership program.

  • Community Library Loan

    Extending library materials and services to local community libraries nationwide.

For Publishers

  • Services to Publishers

    We facilitate ISBN/ISSN registration and coordinate legal deposit of published materials.

For Libraries

  • Publication Supply System

    Support for libraries in sourcing and distributing national publications efficiently.

  • Preservation & Conservation

    Expert services in restoring and maintaining rare and historical documents.

  • Library Collaboration

    Partnerships with public and academic libraries to enhance national knowledge sharing.
